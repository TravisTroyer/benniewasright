2019 Observance

The official Bennie Was Right Day is Tuesday, October 15.

History

On October 15, 2013, Bennie reported a bug in Interlink, with a theory as to why it was not behaving properly. Though we had faith in Bennie, he was surprised when inspection of the code proved his theory correct.

October 15, 2014 was therefore marked as "Bennie Was Right" and is now celebrated each year with cookies.

Official Cookie

The Busken Boosken Iced Cookie is the official cookie of Bennie Was Right Day.

Because Bennie Was Right Day falls so closely to Halloween, it's the perfect way fuel your Halloween spirit!
